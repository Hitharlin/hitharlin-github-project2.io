A GLOBAL BRAND AND CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE AGENCY

In 2018, we united two accomplished marketing and communication companies — VML and Y&R — to deliver a contemporary, fully integrated offering on a global scale. In 2020, VMLY&R joined forces with Geometry to create VMLY&R COMMERCE, a new end-to-end “Creative Commerce Company" that operates as a distinct company within VMLY&R. Today, VMLY&R thrives on creating work that brings people together, united by the power of human connection. We harness creativity, technology, and culture to create connected brands that drive value for our clients and touch lives around the world.

BUILDING CONNECTED BRANDS IN HEALTH

VMLY&R HEALTH is a global communications agency united around a single purpose: improving health all over the world by making science meaningful.


We believe in building connected brands that touch lives, inspire new behaviors and fuel better health. Our award-winning specialist health teams – over 5,000 of them – cover every aspect of the health ecosystem. They combine creativity, technology, insight and behavioral understanding to help brands connect with target audiences to drive meaningful change that improves their health.
